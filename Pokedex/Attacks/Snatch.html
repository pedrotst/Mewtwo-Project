Battle Effect:
The user steals the effects of any healing or stat-changing move the opponent attempts to use.
In-Depth Effect:

Snatch is unique in that it will take the effects of certain moves used by the target and will apply them to the user./ This means the user will get any boost or healing that the opponent intended to use upon itself. The moves it can Snatch are as follows.
